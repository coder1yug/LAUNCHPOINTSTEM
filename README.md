# 🚀 Project Launchpoint STEM
**Empowering the next generation of engineers**

Project Launchpoint STEM teaches kids design and engineering through free classes, ships free build kits to students anywhere in the world, and gives them a platform to create CAD models, compete with peers, and access competitions they never had the chance to enter before.

---

## 🌟 Mission

> Launch Point STEM gives every student — regardless of school budget, location, or background — free access to engineering education, hands-on build kits, and real competition opportunities.

We believe talent is everywhere, but opportunity is not. Our goal is to break down the walls of cost, geography, and access that keep motivated students out of engineering and design.

---

## 🧰 What We Do

- **Free Classes:** Live and recorded lessons teaching engineering fundamentals, mechanical design, and CAD skills to students at any level.
- **Free Build Kits:** Physical robotics and engineering kits designed and shipped to students around the world at no cost, so anyone can build regardless of where they live.
- **CAD & Design Training:** Guided CAD courses that take students from beginner sketches to full working models.
- **Student Competitions:** Our own CAD design and build challenges, giving students a stage to showcase their work and compete against peers globally.
- **Competition Access:** Funding, mentorship, and logistical support to help students reach competitions like [FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC)](https://www.firstinspires.org/robotics/ftc) that would otherwise be out of reach.
- **Mentorship:** Ongoing support from engineers and past students to help teams troubleshoot, improve, and grow.

---

## 💡 Additional Ideas We're Exploring

- **Virtual Design Challenges:** Monthly online CAD prompts so students without a local team can still compete and build a portfolio.
- **Kit Return & Reuse Program:** Students send kits back after their build cycle so materials can be refurbished and shipped to the next student — stretching every dollar further.
- **Alumni Mentor Network:** Past participants return as mentors for new students, creating a self-sustaining pipeline of knowledge.
- **Open CAD Model Library:** A shared archive of student-designed models that future students can learn from and build on.
