# 🚀 Project Launchpoint STEM

**Empowering the next generation of engineers.**

Project Launchpoint STEM funds and mentors underfunded robotics teams, giving students the tools, training, and opportunities to compete in STEM programs like [FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC)](https://www.firstinspires.org/robotics/ftc).

---

## 🌟 Mission

> Launch Point empowers underfunded students with robotics equipment, engineering training, and competition support to launch their futures in STEM.

We believe talent is everywhere, but opportunity is not. Our goal is to make engineering accessible to every motivated student, regardless of their school’s budget.

---

## 🧰 What We Do

- **Equipment Access:** Robotics kits, competition registration, and build materials.  
- **Engineering Training:** CAD bootcamps, design reviews, and technical mentorship.  
- **Competition Support:** Travel funding, tournament support, and team development.  

---

## 📊 Impact Goals

- Fund 2 new robotics teams in 2026  
- Support 30+ students  
- Raise $15,000 in funding  
- Provide 20+ hours of engineering mentorship
